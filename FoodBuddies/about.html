About Food Buddies

Our Mission

Food Buddies aims to reduce food waste, fight hunger, and foster community connections by sharing surplus food resources in a responsible and sustainable way.

Our Core Values

Community

Building a network of individuals and organizations working together to share and care.

Sustainability

Encouraging practices that protect the environment while promoting social responsibility.

Compassion

Providing food resources with kindness, dignity, and care for those in need.
